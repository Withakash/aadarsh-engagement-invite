Product Requirement Document (PRD)
Project: Premium Animated Engagement Invitation Website

Type: Digital Engagement Invitation
Event: Engagement Ceremony
Couple: Adarsh & Harshita

1. Product Overview

This project is a premium digital engagement invitation website designed with a traditional Indian luxury aesthetic. The website will feel like an interactive animated invitation card, combining royal Indian design, cinematic animations, and immersive storytelling.

The website should feel emotional, elegant, and festive, similar to a luxury wedding invitation but specifically for an engagement celebration.

The experience should feel like a story unfolding with scroll-based animations, 3D layered visuals, and smooth transitions between sections.

Important constraints:

This is ONLY an engagement invitation, not a wedding invite.

No RSVP section should exist.

The website must include a dedicated photo story page for the couple.

Background music should play with a play/pause control button.
